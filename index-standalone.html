04 · Economy & income

Work & wealth

From coal mines to Ford, C-Mine and Thor Park — Genk's industrial evolution.

1901

Coal discovered — mines at Zwartberg, Waterschei & Winterslag transform a village of ~2,000 into an industrial city.

1966

Zwartberg mine closes; Winterslag (1986) and Waterschei (1987) follow — post-coal reconversion begins.

2014

Ford Genk Body & Assembly closes (~5,000 jobs at peak) — Mondeo, Galaxy & S-MAX production ends.

Today

Thor Park (195 ha, ~78% occupied) and C-Mine creative hub drive the new economy.
